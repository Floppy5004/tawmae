HOW TO EXPORT MIX IT UP POINTS

1. Go to Currency/Rank/Inventory in your dashboard.

2. Select your currency and click on the Edit button on the right side.

3. In the bottom right corner, click on Export Data.

4. Save the file somewhere you remember. And you're done!

HOW TO EXPORT MIX IT UP POINTS

1. Go to Currency/Rank/Inventory in your dashboard.

2. Select your currency and click on the Edit button on the right side.

3. In the bottom right corner, click on Export Data.

4. Save the file somewhere you remember

5. Since MixItUp allows for custom currencies, you will have to check what the column with your currency is called and then paste that name into the import field for the column name.

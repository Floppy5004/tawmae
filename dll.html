TAWMAEUI DLL

With the TAWMAEUI DLL, you can schedule actions in Streamer.bot to run at specific times. Whether it’s every day at 6 PM, every Thursday at 9 PM, or a one-time trigger at midnight on January 1, 2026—the choice is yours.

3. Copy the downloaded DLL into the dlls folder and restart Streamer.bot.
